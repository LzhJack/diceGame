Instruction:

  • To start, click the 'Start' button.
  • To advance to the next round, click the 'Start' button again.
  • After three rounds, the game is over and the final score will be display with final score area.
  • If you want to play again, click on 'Restart' button or refresh the page.

Rules:

  • Each round, player and computer will both roll a pair of dice.
  • If any players two dice comes up as a 1, the score for this round for the player will be zero.
  • If any players rolls a pair of the same numbers then the player will receive double of the value.
  • Any other combination will get a score for the sum of the two dices they roll.
